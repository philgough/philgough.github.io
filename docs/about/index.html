Dr Phil Gough, FHEA

I’m a Senior Lecturer in Design at The University of Sydney School of Architecture, Design and Planning, in the discipline of design.

I’m also a member of the affective interactions lab, where we design for affective experiences.

Supervision

I am looking to supervise PhD students who are interested in studying a range of aspects of Biodesign, particularly combining digital fabrication and mushroom-based materials and user experience with biomaterials. Please familiarise yourself with USYD’s information about applying and contact me with some idea of what your proposal will be!
